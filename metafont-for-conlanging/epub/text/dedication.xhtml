Dedication

This book began as a rewrite of The METAFONT Tutorial by Christophe Grandsire. There is much of his work that I have copied, in technical details if not in style. Thanks also goes to standard ebooks which served as a basis for how I organized my ebook. The greatest thanks must go to The METAFONT Book by Donald Ervin Knuth, and perusing that will help immensely. We must also thank him as the creator of METAFONT.
