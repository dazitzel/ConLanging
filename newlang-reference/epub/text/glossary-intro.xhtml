Introduction to the English to Newlang Glossary

This is not a dictionary. This is a glossary.

This means that the correct way to use this section of the Newlang Reference is to look up a word as a indication of where to find the information you really want to use.

It is not safe to look up a word and use that as a translation.

If you can't find a word, synonyms or a good thesaurus may help.
